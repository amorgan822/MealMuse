Weekly Dish: Tacos

Tacos are a delicious and versatile Mexican dish consisting of a corn or wheat tortilla folded or rolled around a filling. They can be filled with various ingredients, making them perfect for customization according to your taste preferences.

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas (flour or corn)
  • Protein: Ground beef, Chicken, Shrimp, Fish, Beans
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
